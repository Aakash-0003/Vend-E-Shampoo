Working of our Model

First, we're planning to collaborate with the big shampoo brands and make our proposal for the betterment of the society by explaining every bit and byte around the plastic pollution , climate change and how it's affecting , not only the marine and ecosystem , but directly harming our health and proposing them the idea of manufacturing non plastic bottles to store shampoo, conditioners,etc , in place of the plastic ones.

Then, We'll be proposing our plan and approach and will try to influence the authorities , government and the common people through various platforms to adapt this approach and to use the non-plastic bottles ,e.g. aluminium or steel bottles, in place of plastic bottles and make their contribution for this humanity saving cause and the betterment of the society.

and then, We'll be proposing our model and will encourage the authorities and people to use this approach, to tackle this big time battle of the drastic climate change coming our way, by using the refillable non plastic bottles and get it refilled by the Vend-E-Shampoo machine ,installed at the local shops in thier locality.
The Machine will be managed and operated manually by the shopkeeper ,in the beginning phase of this project,where the Token will be provided by the shopkeeper to the customer to access the machine where the customers can refilled their non-plastic shampoo bottles, according to their preferred brands, labelled on the machine .
